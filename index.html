Comprehensive IPO Advisory

From pre-IPO structuring to post-listing compliance, our full-spectrum advisory covers every milestone of your public market journey.

01
IPO Readiness Assessment
Comprehensive evaluation of financial health, corporate governance, related party transactions, and regulatory posture to confirm IPO-readiness before committing.
→
02
DRHP / RHP Preparation
Expert drafting of Draft Red Herring Prospectus under SEBI ICDR Regulations, coordinating with legal counsel, statutory auditors, and merchant bankers.
→
03
SEBI Compliance & Filing
End-to-end SEBI observation letter management, query responses, ICDR and LODR regulatory guidance, and listing application filing with NSE / BSE.
→
04
Financial Restructuring
Pre-IPO capital structure optimization, balance sheet cleanup, related-party unwinding, ESOP scheme design, and financial model preparation for SEBI filing.
→
05
Due Diligence Management
Coordinated legal, financial, and operational due diligence with merchant bankers, IPO grading agencies, statutory auditors, and legal advisors.
→
06
Investor Relations & Roadshow

→
07
Book Building & Pricing
Strategic price band determination, book building process oversight, anchor investor coordination, and grey market premium monitoring for optimal listing.
→
08
Post-IPO Compliance
Ongoing SEBI LODR compliance, quarterly results disclosures, related party transaction approvals, corporate governance advisory, and ESOP exercise support.
